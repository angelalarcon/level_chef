Sushi - Advanced techniques

In this level of making sushi, we will explain how to perfect the sushi rice to its maximum, and teach how to make the nigiri by the vertical flip method (tategaseshi) method, making a thick roll (futomaki), and the outside roll (sotomaki). You will blend your own sushi rice vinegar from scratch and use Japanese short grain rice to make the best sushi rice possible. Japanese charcoal and kelp will be used to further refine the taste of the rice. .
